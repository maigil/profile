STORY OF MY LIFE

MAI GIL

POINTS

My lovely dog

I am raising a cute dog named Aki.
I love her very much.

Family

I was born in Japan to a Japanese mother and an Israeli father.
We immigrated to Israel when I was young so most of my life I live in Israel.
I grew up in a family of 5 people and I am the little sister among 3 sisters.

Travel

I really like traveling in Israel and abroad.
I love trekking and see spectacular views of nature.

Army

I served in the I.D.F for five and a half years, of which almost four years as an officer.
I served as an HR officer in a number of diverse roles.

Gymnastics

Before I joined the army I was a gymnast for 14 years.
Over the years, I competed in competitions in Israel and around the world and was even the champion of Israel in 2013.
Even today, sport is a very beloved and important part of my life.

Art

I love the field of art - design, painting, creation and styling.
Art is an integral part of my life and personality.

Music

When I was little I studied piano for 9 years, during which I also performed in small concerts.
Even today, I find the music very relaxing and not a day goes by that I don't listen to music.
In addition, I still like to play and sing as a hobby.
